# Dialtone Social Skill
Dialtone Social provides community and social sharing workflows for robot moments and collaborative events. It focuses on safe sharing, permissions, and multi-user interactions.

## Core Focus
- Share robot moments to social platforms.
- Support multi-user collaborative robot events.
- Provide safe, moderated public engagement tools.

## Capabilities
- One-click sharing for robot media and updates.
- Collaborative "Robot Parties" for shared interactions.
- Social engagement tracking and moderation hooks.

## Inputs
- Media assets and event metadata.
- User roles and sharing permissions.
- Target platforms and publishing settings.

## Outputs
- Shared posts and public links.
- Participation metrics and audit logs.
- Moderation reports or alerts.

## Workflows
This skill can use many workflows, including:
- `docs/workflows/ticket.md` for social features.
- `docs/workflows/subtask_expand.md` for sharing flows.
- `docs/workflows/issue_review.md` for moderation bugs.

## Plugins
This skill can use many plugins, including:
- `./dialtone.sh www` and `./dialtone.sh ui` for social experiences.
- `./dialtone.sh logs` for tracking and audit.
- `./dialtone.sh github` for issue linkage and planning.

## Example Tasks
- Add a sharing template for mission highlights.
- Create a multi-user session flow with permissions.
- Instrument engagement metrics for robot events.

## Notes
- Respect privacy settings by default.
- Keep moderation and audit trails enabled.
